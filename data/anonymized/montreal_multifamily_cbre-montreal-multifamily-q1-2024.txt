Submarket Boundaries
Definitions
Age Category:
• New Build: For buildings built within the last 5 years relative to the transaction year (e.g., 2024-2019 for a
transaction in 2024).
• 2000s Build: For buildings built between 2000 and the year prior to what is classified as “New Build.”
• Old Build: For buildings built before 2000.
Housing affordability Index: The measure is a ratio of housing-related costs (mortgage payments and utility fees)
to average household disposable income.
Survey Criteria
CBRE’s market report analyses apartment buildings of 8 units and greater and a minimum threshold of $1 million
dollars across the GMA. CBRE assembles all information through in-house research and third-party data collection.
