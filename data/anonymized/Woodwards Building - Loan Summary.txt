MARKET RENT COMMENTARY – OFFICE:
Office lease rates in downtown Vancouver vary depending on several key factors including building class,
location, age and condition, tenant profile, lease terms, and inducements offered. Higher lease rates are
typically achieved in newer, well-located buildings with strong amenities and efficient layouts, particularly
those situated along major transit corridors or within the downtown core. These properties often attract
government, institutional and professional service tenants who value accessibility and building quality.
Conversely, lower lease rates are generally associated with older or less functional buildings, secondary
locations, or larger floor plates that may be less flexible for multi-tenant configurations. In the current market,
tenant inducements such as tenant improvement allowances and free rent periods remain common,
particularly for larger spaces or those requiring significant build-out.

the assumptions used in the prior appraisal, market rent for the subject’s vacant office space is estimated at
$30 / s.f. NNN. This estimate reflects the subject’s location with the [PERSON] area, its modern construction,
