Rent excludes one or more of the ”net” costs (real property taxes, building insurance, and major maintenance) typically
included in a Gross Lease Rate. Vacancy Rate: Total vacant sq. ft. divided by the total building area. Vacant sq. ft.:
Space that can be occupied within 30 days. Availability: All current and future marketed lease availabilities in existing
inventory. Full floor: a floor over 8,000 sq. ft.
